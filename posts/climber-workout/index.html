Movement’s Quarantine Workout for Rebalancing and Rebuilding:

B: BEGINNER, I: INTERMEDIATE, A : ADVANCE

  1. PUSHUPS
    • Warmup - External Rotations x10 and 5 slow pushups.
    • B: Kneeling Push-ups 3 sets 10 reps
    • I: Normal Pushups 3 sets 10 reps
    • A: Lizard Pushups 3 sets 10 reps (5per leg)
  1. HANDSTAND WALKS
    • Warmup - Wrist Circles 8 each direction and Downward Dog
    • B: 1 Walk in&out with 10 sec hold
    • I: 2 Walks with 3 sec hold
    • A: 3 Walks with 3 sec hold
  2. BRIDGE ROTATIONS
    • Warmup - Crab Pose 20 sec and Sphinx 20 sec
    • B: Wall Rotations 3 sets 6 reps (3 per arm)
    • I: Floor 3/4 Rotations 3 sets 6 reps
    • A: Floor Full Rotations 3 sets 6 reps
  3. TUCK PLANCHE Warmup - 10 Scapular Pushups B: Chair/Stool w/ Feet Below Hands 3 sets 10 secs I: Feet lvl w/ Hands 3 sets 10 secs A: Hands on Floor High Feet 3 sets 10 secs
  4. L-SITS Warmup - Active Forward Fold 30 secs B:Chair/Stool Tuck Sit w/ knees tucked high 3 sets 10 secs I: Low Legs Straight 3 sets 10 secs A: Legs Straight lvl w/ Hands 3 sets 10 secs
  5. PISTOL SQUATS Warmup - 10 slow Full Range Squats and End Range Holds 10 sec per leg x2 B: Rolling to End Range + Squat 3 sets 10 reps (5 per leg) I: Rolling to Stand 3 sets 10 reps (5/leg) A: Pistol Squats 3 sets 10 reps (5/leg)
  6. PULL UPS Warmup - Scapular Shrugs x 5 reps B: Jumping Negatives 3 sets 3 reps I & A: Pull Ups 3 sets 6-10 reps
